Bejeweled Blitz: Aquamarine

Description:

In the match 3 game, Bejeweled Blitz, Aquamarine is a rare gem that clears gems

on the board when matched with 2 other blue gems. Visually, Aquamarine appears to

resemble the top of Poseidon's trident. In addition to clearing gems, Aquamarine

transforms nearby red and orange gems into blue gems. When the player begins their

one-minute game with Aquamarine activated, Aquamarine gems will be configured randomly

accross the board. At the end of the game, players can choose a prize to win coins!
